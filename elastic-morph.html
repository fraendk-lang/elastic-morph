Elastic Morph — Quick Guide

Get started

Drop an audio file on the canvas (or Upload Audio). The visual is born from your sound, analyses the song structure and evolves across it. No file? 🎤 Live Input visualises your mic / line-in.

Layers (stackable)

⚡ Shader Engine = GPU-rendered light bed (Fluid, Metaballs/Plasma, Tunnel/Kaleidoscope) — toggle with G. Visual DNA = the core organism (10 presets, blend two of them). Particle Mode = 8 patterns. Layer B = 16 geometric/spectral overlays. Image A & B = your photos, modulated, with Ken Burns + A↔B interplay. FX Rack I = 10 pixel/colour effects (keys 1–0); FX Rack II = 10 geometry/space effects (Ctrl+1–0).

Text & Lyrics

Creator Cuts → Text Designer (style, animation). The Lyrics tab imports .lrc or tap-syncs your own lines to the beat.

Keyboard

Space Play / Pause ← → Seek ±5s   Home Restart 1–9, 0 Toggle FX rack I Ctrl+1–0 Toggle FX rack II (geometry) Shift+1–4 Recall scene A–D T Tap-sync a lyric line G Toggle GPU shader M Stamp a set marker P Save still (PNG) F Fullscreen   B Blackout ? This help   Esc Close

Perform & export

Scenes (Settings) store whole looks for live switching. Auto-VJ evolves everything hands-off. Set Editor (Creator Cuts) lets you place timed markers on a big waveform that recall scenes + FX — it plays back deterministically, including in the export. ● Export records video+audio; Save Still grabs a PNG. Keep the window visible while recording.

Not seeing changes?

Hard-reload with Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + R.

🎬 Set Editor

Click or drag the waveform to scrub. Press M (while the track plays) to stamp a marker at the playhead — markers snap to the beat. Select a marker to assign a scene (A–D) and optionally capture the current FX racks as an override. The set plays back deterministically, including in the export.
